Top 5 Favourite Movies

  1. Gilli
    • Year Released: 2004
    • Main Actors: Vijay, Trisha, Prakash Raj
    • Description: Saravanavelu "Velu", a state-level Kabaddi player living in Chennai with his family, is constantly rebuked by his father, DCP Sivasubramaniam for neglecting his studies and favouring Kabaddi, Velu is sent to his relative's wedding in Trichy, but he secretly skips the wedding to play a Kabaddi match in Madurai.
  2. Jayam
    • Year Released: 2002
    • Main Actors: Jayam Ravi, Sadha
    • Description: Venkataramana alias "Venky" is from a poor family who meets Sujatha at his college and falls in love with her. where Venky finally defeats Raghu. With Sujatha's father's approval, Venky and Sujatha get married in presence of Raghu and her family members.
  3. Vikram
    • Year Released: 2022
    • Main Actors: Kamal Haasan, Vijay Sethupathi, Fahadh Faasil
    • Description: Amar, the head of a black-ops squad is summoned by Police commissioner Jose to bring justice to a group of masked vigilantes for the murder of Stephen Raj. Vikram is present at the meeting and walks away with determination after learning about the bounty placed on all of them.
  4. The Shawshank Redemption
    • Year Released: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman, Bob Gunton
    • Description: Two imprisoned men form a deep bond over the years, finding solace and redemption through acts of common decency.
  5. Forrest Gump
    • Year Released: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tom Hanks, Robin Wright, Gary Sinise
    • Description: The story of Forrest Gump, a man with a low IQ, who recounts the events of his extraordinary life, including the significant impact he had on several historical events.
